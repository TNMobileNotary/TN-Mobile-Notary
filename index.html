About TN Mobile Notary

Naté A. Johnson

Licensed & Bonded Notary Public

With over 15 years of experience in the notary field, Naté A. Johnson brings professionalism, attention to detail, and genuine care to every client interaction.

Specializing in real estate transactions, estate planning documents, and loan signings, Naté understands the importance of accuracy and confidentiality in notarizing your critical documents.

What sets TN Mobile Notary apart is our commitment to making the notarization process as smooth and stress-free as possible. We come to you, whether it's your home, office, hospital, or even correctional facility.

"My goal is to provide notary services with the same care and attention I would want for my own family's important documents."

- Naté A. Johnson

Our Services

Real Estate Closings

Professional notarization for all real estate transactions including purchase agreements, refinances, and loan documents.

Wills & Trusts

Secure notarization for your estate planning documents to ensure they're legally binding and properly executed.

Power of Attorney

Discreet and professional handling of sensitive power of attorney documents for healthcare or financial matters.

Apostilles / Birth Certificates

Certification of documents for international use, including birth certificates, marriage licenses, and academic records.

Travel Consent

Official notarization of parental or guardian consent documents for minors traveling domestically or internationally, ensuring legal compliance & peace of mind.

General Notarizations

All standard notary services including affidavits, acknowledgments, jurats, and copy certifications.
